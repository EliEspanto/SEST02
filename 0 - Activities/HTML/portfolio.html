    Favorite Food: Tinolang Manok

    Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large unripe papaya, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup green papaya leaves
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 2-3 pieces finger chili, sliced (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Water, as needed

    • Instructions:

    1. In a large pot, heat a little oil over medium heat. Saut√© the onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
    2. Add the chicken pieces to the pot and brown them lightly on all sides.
    3. Pour enough water to cover the chicken. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the chicken is tender.
    4. Add the unripe papaya, green papaya leaves, and chili peppers to the pot. Simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the papaya is tender.
    5. Season with fish sauce and salt to taste.
